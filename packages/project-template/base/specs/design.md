# Design Spec

## Style

Modern corporate, clear, and trustworthy. The site should feel like a practical training provider, not a consumer app or marketing gimmick.

## Visual System

- Clean typography with strong hierarchy.
- Responsive layout for desktop and mobile.
- Accessible form labels and readable contrast.
- Strong CTA color for workshop requests.
- Restrained use of cards for individual program items.
- Avoid visual clutter and oversized decorative sections.

## Tone

Confident, specific, and training-company focused. Copy should describe outcomes, not vague AI excitement.

## Components

- Header with brand and CTA.
- Hero with a direct offer.
- Program cards.
- Why choose us columns.
- Lead form with clear labels and validation.
